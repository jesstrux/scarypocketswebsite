Reviving da funk. whatcha say?

We are a funk band that releases weekly music videos(normally on Mondays). Scary Pockets is Ryan Lerman and Jack Conte, with the help and support from a rotating roster of the best session musicians in the LA area.

Ryan and Jack

Ryan and Jack have been best friends since college. They met on the freshman musical intervention at berkley where they geeked out about music concepts that fascinated them, Jack going on and on about how the keys have saved him and Ryan insisting that bass is the s**t. After a while they found some common ground in their love and respect for Stevie Wonder, The Fugees, Chaka Khan and their absolute favorite James Brown.



Scary Pockets

For a long while Ryan and Jack had been toying with the idea of 'bringing back the good ole funk days' by creating modernized funk experiences for the old timers and new school alike. The problem however was how to create an experience that was going to be relatable for both. One day when Ryan was watching an acoustic cover video on youtube, he called Jack shouting, "I KNOW HOW WE'LL DO IT, I KNOW HOW WE'LL DO IT" and proceeded to tell him.



First Session

When they decided they were both serious about the funk pursuit idea. They called India Carney who was a phenomenal singer(she did opera, what more can we say?) and who had just gained a huge fanbase from her time on The Voice. They tried out some songs until they came to Creep which just made them smile when they played it back. So they chose that as their debut single and hit the internet to see it makes of their cover and much to their suprise they had way more views than some cat videos(not the very famous ones of course) and the rest like they say(whoever they are) is history.
